=========================
 Media and entertainment
=========================

.. admonition:: Description

        mFabrik Web and Mobile combines
        text, image, audio and video content 
        management to a single integrated process.
        This enables efficient management even big 
        media sites.

With integrated video and audio encoding 
servers, mFabrik Web and Mobile can 
input any video format and create
different transcodes it for various 
devices: web site Flash and HTML5,
mobile stream and download. The video
files can be syncrhonized with your brand's 
YouTube channel or other video service.

Live streaming allows
instant sharing of your events in web and mobile.
Live events can be combined with real-time chat or Twitter feed. 
Your users can share their own broadcasts
through webcams.

Features
----------

* Transcoding of video files to different medias: Flash video, h264, 3GP

* Streaming and download: RTSP and HTTP progressive download

* Device support: Internet browsers since IE6, Apple devices,
  smart phones, feature phones

* Real-time chat (Javascript / Flash) and Twitter
