=========================
 mFabrik Web and Mobile
=========================

mFabrik Web and Mobile is a publishing platform 
integrating multiple channels to one, easy to use,
editor system. It allows you to create
sites which gives the best reach and user 
experience.  

* Web sites

* Mobile sites

* Email 

* SMS

* Social media

* Mobile applications

Use Cases
-----------

Travel sites
=============
Travellers need to be served
before travel, during the trip (mobile)
and after sales service (email, SMS).

Intra- end extranets
======================

mFabrik Web and Mobile provides comprehensive 
personalization tools to 
cut information overload.

Deliver business critical information to 
your organization members on road over 
mobile web and SMS.


Campaign sites
================

Maximize your advertise campaign potential 
by combining web, mobile and print.
Integrate sites to CRM to automatically
collect and refine leads. Social network
connection automatically delivers
updates to fans.

mFabrik Web and Mobile enables 
the creation of stunning HTML5 sites
which also work on iPad and other
media devices. Create offline applications
from the content to raise brand awarness
in the mobile channel. 

Media and entertainment
=========================

Need to tap the potential of video,
on mobile too? mFabrik Web and Mobile
makes sure your video files plays in 
on every digital media.

Advanced content management features ensure
that even huge media archives are easily accessible
regardless of the user device.
