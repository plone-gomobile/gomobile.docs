==========================
 About the product
==========================

.. admonition:: Description

        mFabrik Web and Mobile is a publishing platform
        allowing you to distribute personalized information 
        through various channels. It's solves
        the problem of managing different medias and 
        information overload of the contemporary world.

Vision
-------

The future of digital communications is 
about more personalized interaction where  
the user is not chained to the desktop. 
Instead of seeking the information, 
the information where are interested arrives
to us. 

Use cases
-----------

mFabrik Web and Mobile streamlines the process
of digital information delivery. 
Create information once and then 
use various push and pull channels to 
distribute and interact with it.

More information sensitive and more
internet aware your business, the better mFabrik Web and Mobile 
solutions suits for it. Some vertical segments
cover business applications (intra- and extranets),
travel industry solutions, campaigns and 
media sites.

Services and support
---------------------

mFabrik Web and Mobile is a complex product.
mFabrik wants to help you to get most of it
by providing different services options.
Whether you are a developer only needs to ensure 
the code works for you or a organization needing
a turn key solution, we want make sure your 
business runs smoothly.


High quality open source product
--------------------------------

mFabrik Web and Mobile is built on the open source 
technologies with the promise of quality of transparency. 
The customer is never trapped in a situation where where the further
development or switching the vendor where not possible.
